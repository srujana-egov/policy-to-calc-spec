# Fixture A — Chennai Corporation trade licence fee schedule

## SCHEDULE-I — Micro Cottage Industries
Trades 1–47 (Plastic works, Tailoring Machine, Laundry (By Power), ... Spray painting and Water
Service): banded by area —
- Up to 1000 sq.ft.: Rs. 2000
- Above 1000 sq.ft.: Rs. 5000

Trades 48–81 (Arc. Welding, Wet Flour Grinding, ... Interior decorating goods sales): 3-band —
- Upto 500 sq.ft.: Rs. 500
- From 501 sq.ft. to 1000 sq.ft.: Rs. 750
- Above 1000 sq.ft.: Rs. 5000

Trade 82 — Petrol Bunk with Service station: **two separately listed fees, not a band** —
- Petrol Bunk: Rs. 6000
- Service station: Rs. 3000

## SCHEDULE-II — Industries manufacturing and dealing with commercial item
Trades 1–40 (Chemical selling retail ... Tyre Shop): flat, no banding —
- Rs. 5000

## SCHEDULE-III — Shop dealing with commercial products (small scale)
Trades 1–21 (Storing of Paints ... Sewing material sales): flat, no banding —
- Rs. 500

## SCHEDULE-IV — Shop dealing with food articles
Trades 1–16 (Coffee Roasting & Grinding ... Vegetable & Fruit Sales): 2-band by area —
- Upto 1000 sq.ft.: Rs. 500
- Above 1000 sq.ft.: Rs. 1500

## SCHEDULE-V — Trades dealing with food and daily essentials
- Provision Store, S.M. Stall, Bakery: 3-band by area — ≤500: Rs. 500; 501–1000: Rs. 750; >1000: Rs. 3000
- Tea Stall / Chicken, Mutton, Beef and Fish Stalls: 2-band by area — ≤500: Rs. 1000; >500: Rs. 2000
- Eating House/Fast Food/Canteen/Restaurant/Departmental Stores: 2-band by area — ≤1000: Rs. 2500; >1000: Rs. 10000

## SCHEDULE-VI — General
Trades 1–12 (Carpentry ... Sticker Shop): flat, no banding — Rs. 500
Trade 13 — Hair cutting saloon: banded by a category, not area —
- Non A.C.: Rs. 200
- With A.C.: Rs. 500
